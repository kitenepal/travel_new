MEETINGS & CONFERENCES

At Travelite (India) we ensure your meeting / conference is handled personally and professionally by our expert MICE Team, who are accessible and accountable to you at all times.

We create customized conference solutions for your client to suit their requirements and preferences. When we start working on your meeting or conference we will discuss in detail about the nature of conference, profile of client, expectation from the destination and venue, production style, set design, audio visual requirements, breakout room requirements etc to suggest a conference solution that is most appropriate to meet your clients request.

From initial stages of venue search through to post conference evaluation we adopt a comprehensive approach to ensure all aspects of the conference are concurrently reviewed and assessed. We understand and appreciate the inherent differences between different types of conferences such as a sales kick off meeting, a pharma training, a financial forecast meeting or a bankers conference – each conference is approached with an industry specific perspective to ensure we deliver on expectations.

EVENTS

At Travelite (India), our events team is dedicated to assist you in planning and organizing your event with utmost attention to detail. We have ideas to suit different budgets and expectations. We understand that every event has a specific purpose, a target audience and a definite expectation of results. We work with you in understanding various aspects of the event to ensure our suggestions most appropriately meet your requirements.

Our team is experienced in organizing a diverse range of events such as Corporate Award Ceremonies, Product or Brand Launch, Theme Events, Dealer Incentives, Sales Team Events, Executive Retreats & Incentives, Team Building Events, Trade Fairs & Exhibitions, High Security Celebrity Parties, Road Shows, Press Meets, Real or Mock Weddings, Sports Events.

We believe that for the success of any event, planning is paramount. A well planned event should function like clock work. We spend a fair amount of time working with you on planning stage of the event. All aspects are discussed in detail, roadmaps are drafted, timelines are established, responsibilities are set and production commences only once all details are confirmed in black and white.

EXHIBITION & DELEGATIONS

India is one of the fastest growing economies of the world. Today our country receives great interest from around the world. We see trade and business delegations visiting India to study the market, meet suppliers, foster new partnerships, understand the mindset of the country with a view to develop India as a new market or supply base.

We understand that when on business, time is critical. Delegations may vary from Private Investor Groups, Government Ministerial Delegations, Industrial Site Visits or Trade Delegations through Foreign Embassies and High Commissions. In all cases, time and security are paramount.

Handling a delegation calls for expert organization abilities, long standing experience to function seamlessly across India, have a panel of industry specific translators in various languages, ability to foresee potential glitches and fix them beforehand and most of all a well connected team with relevant experience.

Organizing a successful delegation can sometimes be a challenge, but when we look back the experience has been rewarding. Today our team is equipped with a skill set hard to find.
